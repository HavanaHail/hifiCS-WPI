CS @ WPI

CS @ WPI is a collection of resources for Computer Science students at WPI to help them succeed in their studies and classes.
This data was gathered from students enrolled in a variety of
advanced computer science courses and categorized into three different buckets: Design, Systems and Theory.
Following the WPI Computer Science Course Flow Take a look around and see what there is to do! Assuming that we are missing something, go ahead and propose your own resource to the right.
